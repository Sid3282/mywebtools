Astrology Tools

Nepali Astrology tools provide daily horoscope (rashifal) and Panchanga information based on Vedic astrology. These tools are deeply rooted in Nepali culture and are widely consulted for important life events.

Features include:

  • Daily horoscope for all zodiac signs
  • Detailed Panchanga with tithi, nakshatra, yoga and karana
  • Auspicious dates and times (shubha muhurta)
  • Planetary positions and their effects

  • सबै राशिहरूको लागि दैनिक राशिफल
