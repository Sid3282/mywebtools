Gold & Silver Prices

Gold holds significant cultural and economic importance in Nepal. The price of gold in Nepal fluctuates daily based on international market rates, exchange rates, and local demand.

Our gold price tool provides:

  • Today's gold price in Nepalese Rupees (NPR)
  • Prices per tola (11.664 grams) and per gram
  • Silver prices updated daily
  • Price trends and historical data
  • Comparison between buying and selling prices

Note: Prices are indicative and may vary between jewelers.
