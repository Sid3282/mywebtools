Date Converter

The Nepali Date Converter tool allows you to easily convert dates between the Bikram Sambat (BS) and Gregorian (AD) calendars. This is particularly useful for:

  • Converting your birth date from AD to BS or vice versa
  • Understanding Nepali festival dates in the Gregorian calendar
  • Planning events that need coordination between both calendar systems
  • Historical date conversions for research purposes

Simply select the date you want to convert and the tool will instantly show you the corresponding date in the other calendar system.
