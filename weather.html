Nepal Weather Observation

Nepal's weather varies dramatically from the tropical south to the arctic north due to its extreme altitude variations. Our weather tool provides accurate and up-to-date weather information for major locations across Nepal.

Features include:

  • Current temperature and conditions
  • Hourly and daily forecasts
  • Rainfall and humidity levels
  • Wind speed and direction
  • Sunrise and sunset times

This information is particularly useful for travelers, trekkers, and those planning outdoor activities in Nepal.
